1. **my-ny-business.jpg** - MY NY Brands business photo

## Website Screenshots (for Black Orchyd, AI Wear, Agnes, Auro)
Take screenshots of the websites for these brands:

4. **black-orchyd-website.jpg** - Black Orchyd website screenshot
5. **ai-wear-website.jpg** - AI Wear website screenshot
6. **agnes-website.jpg** - Agnes website screenshot
7. **auro-website.jpg** - Auro website screenshot

## Tips for Best Results:
- **Business photos**: Use high-quality images showing your products/storefront/team
- **Website screenshots**: Capture the homepage or most impressive page
- **Format**: JPG or PNG format
- **Size**: Recommended 1200px width minimum for good quality
- **Aspect ratios**: 
  - Business photos: Square (1:1) works best
  - Website screenshots: Landscape (16:9) works best

Once you add these images to the `/public` folder, they will automatically display on the portfolio page!
